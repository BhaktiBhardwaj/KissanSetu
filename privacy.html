Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Our Commitment to Farmers

At KissanSetu, we understand that farmers data is sensitive and crucial to their livelihood. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your information is used solely to provide you with fair crop pricing information and market transparency.

How We Use Your Information

  • Fair Pricing: To provide accurate, real-time crop pricing information
  • Market Access: To connect you with legitimate buyers and cooperatives
  • Personalization: To show relevant crops and regional market data
  • Protection: To identify and prevent unfair pricing practices
  • Communication: To send important price alerts and market updates

Information We Collect

Personal information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Location and farming area details
  • Crop types and farming practices
  • Transaction history and pricing data

Usage information

  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Device information and IP address
  • Search queries and market interactions

Data Protection for Farmers

Your Rights

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data
  • Objection: Opt out of certain data processing activities

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive farming data
  • Secure servers with regular security audits
  • Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular data backups and recovery procedures

Sharing Information

We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

Limited Sharing for Your Benefit

  • Verified Buyers: Only necessary contact info when you initiate a transaction
  • Agricultural Cooperatives: To help you access better group pricing
  • Government Schemes: To help you access subsidies and support programs (with consent)
  • Research: Anonymized data to study unfair pricing patterns

Multilingual Support & Local Privacy Laws

We comply with local data protection laws in all regions where we operate. Our privacy policy is available in multiple Indian languages to ensure farmers can understand their rights in their preferred language.
